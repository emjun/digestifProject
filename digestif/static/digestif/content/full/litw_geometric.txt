<h1>Results</h1>
<p>In this experiment you were asked to reproduce a line in a box relative to the size of the box, or a line independent of the size of the box.
  We are interested in finding out whether people are better in one of these two tasks, and whether this relates to their cultural background.</p>
<p>Please see your results on this test below. If you have any questions about these results, please email us at info@labinthewild.org.</p>
